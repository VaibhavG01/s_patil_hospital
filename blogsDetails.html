❤️ Heart Health Tips: Simple Habits That Save Lives

Dr. Neha Bhangdiya Biyani
• Published on June 15, 2025

Maintaining a healthy heart isn't as complex as many think. With a few consistent lifestyle changes, your cardiovascular system can remain strong well into your later years.

1. Eat a Balanced Diet

Incorporate fresh fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains. Limit your intake of salt, sugar, and saturated fats.

2. Stay Active

At least 30 minutes of moderate exercise (like brisk walking or cycling) five times a week significantly improves heart health.

3. Manage Stress

Chronic stress can increase blood pressure and lead to unhealthy behaviors. Practice yoga, meditation, or deep breathing regularly.

4. Avoid Tobacco and Limit Alcohol

Smoking is one of the biggest risk factors for heart disease. Avoid smoking and limit alcohol to recommended levels.

“Your heart is the engine of your body — take care of it, and it will take care of you.” — Dr. Neha Bhangdiya Biyani

Regular check-ups with your physician can catch warning signs early. Prevention is always better than cure. Stay healthy, stay informed!
